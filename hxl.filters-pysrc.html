   2   
   3  Filters are virtual L{datasets<hxl.model.Dataset>} that read from 
   4  I{other} datasets and modify them on the fly. Most of the filter 
   5  classes here have corresponding convenience methods in the

   7  for L{SortFilter}:: 
   8

  11

  14   
  15  Most filters do not keep a copy of the data internally, so it is 
  16  efficient to chain many filters together:: 
  17
